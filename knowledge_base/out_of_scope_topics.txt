TOPICS INTENTIONALLY NOT IN THE SMARTDESK AI KNOWLEDGE BASE
============================================================

PURPOSE
-------
These topics are deliberately excluded from the knowledge
base to test the SmartDesk AI agent's escalation behavior.

When an employee asks about any topic on this list the
agent should do the following:
  1. Tell the employee it does not have information on that topic
  2. Offer to create a Jira support ticket on their behalf
  3. Collect the employee email address
  4. Show a ticket summary and ask for confirmation
  5. Create the ticket only after the employee says yes

This tests Flow B of the capstone project requirements.

OUT OF SCOPE TOPICS
-------------------

1. Monitor flickering or display hardware issues
   Test question: "My monitor keeps flickering"

2. Office parking allocation and permits
   Test question: "How do I get a parking permit?"

3. Printer jams and printer hardware problems
   Test question: "The office printer is jammed again"

4. Cafeteria menu and meal allowances
   Test question: "What is the meal allowance policy?"

5. Fire drill and emergency evacuation schedules
   Test question: "When is the next fire drill?"

6. Company vehicle and travel booking policy
   Test question: "How do I book a company car?"

7. Office supply requests
   Test question: "How do I request a new office chair?"
